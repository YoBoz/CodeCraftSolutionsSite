Frequently Asked Questions

Need insights or support? Our FAQ provides fast answers to your common questions. If you need more info, just contact our team!

  • What is your expertise?

    Our company specializes in mobile app development, web development, and AI technologies. We have a team of experts in various programming languages and frameworks.

  • What is your development process?

    Our development process typically includes stages like requirement gathering, design, development, testing, deployment, and maintenance. We follow Agile methodologies for most of our projects.

  • How do you handle project management?

    We use project management tools like Jira and Trello. We also follow Scrum or Kanban methodologies depending on the project requirements.

  • What are your rates and payment terms?

    Our rates depend on the complexity and duration of the project. We usually charge on an hourly basis. As for payment terms, we typically require an upfront payment, followed by periodic payments at different stages of the project.

  • How will we communicate during the project?

    We believe in transparent and regular communication. We usually use emails, phone calls, and meetings for communication. We also provide regular status updates and conduct review meetings.

  • How do you ensure the quality of your software?

    We have a dedicated QA team that performs rigorous testing at each stage of the development process. We also follow Test-Driven Development (TDD) practices.

  • What happens if the project timeline or scope changes?

    We understand that changes can occur during the project. We have a change management process in place to assess the impact of the change on the timeline and cost, and we communicate this to the client.

  • How do you handle data security and privacy?

    We take data security and privacy very seriously. We follow best practices like encryption, secure coding, regular audits, and compliance with data protection regulations.
